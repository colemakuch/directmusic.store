TAKE ME HOME

    This site is a DIY algorithm-free and metric-free resource allowing fans to purchase music from artists directly, with no fees or cuts taken by payment processing or streaming platforms.
    It also provides artists a stable web presence and link to host their music and payment options.
    It was inspired by Cindy Lee's choice to only stream their album Diamond Jubilee on youtube, and host the music for download and purchase on this site.
    This site is built on trust and good faith. We basically just host links, and trust that arists and fans play nicely.
    Please try to resolve issues independently, but concerns can be directed here.
